Business Value & Impact - Lean Canvas

A strategic overview of the FCW-Pro project, outlining its core components from problem to market impact.

Problem

  • High complexity and time consumption in developing FCW systems.
  • Difficulty achieving compliance with stringent standards like MISRA & ASPICE.
  • Inefficiencies in manual testing and validation.
  • Limited adaptability to diverse weather/road conditions.

Solution

  • Hybrid AI approach combining LLMs for adaptive parameter optimization and YOLOv11n for object detection.
  • Fully integrated simulation testing environment using CARLA.
  • Scalable framework for expanding to other ADAS features.

Value Propositions

  • 200% faster development with GenAI-powered code generation.
  • 95% object detection accuracy with a scalable, modular architecture.
  • Real-time weather-adaptive collision detection for enhanced safety.
  • MISRA-C compliance adherence.
  • Comprehensive testing framework (CARLA, Unity, Gazebo).

Customer Segments

  • Automotive OEMs looking to accelerate ADAS development.
  • Tier-1 suppliers seeking compliance-ready solutions.
  • Autonomous vehicle companies exploring advanced collision detection.
  • Regulatory bodies evaluating new safety tech.

Channels

  • Automotive OEMs
  • Tier-1 Suppliers
  • Technology Conferences
  • Publications & Online Platforms

Revenue Stream

  • Licensing of DriveWise software.
  • Subscription model for ongoing updates and support.
  • Custom implementation and consulting.
  • Collaboration for technology adoption programs.

Cost Structure

  • Development Costs: R&D for AI model training, simulation environment setup, and hardware.
  • Compliance & Testing: Certification for MISRA, ASPICE, and functional safety.
  • Operational Costs: Cloud services, simulation maintenance, and API usage.
  • Marketing & Outreach: Participation in conferences, advertising, and publications.

Current Market Competition

  • Competitors: Mobileye, Bosch, Continental.
  • Differentiators: Faster development, hybrid GenAI-CV, real-time optimization.
  • Trends: AI-ADAS growth, V2X demand.
